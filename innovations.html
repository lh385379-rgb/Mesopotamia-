Mesopotamians created many inventions that changed human history. One of the most important was writing, which allowed people to keep records and pass down knowledge.

They also invented the wheel, which improved transportation and trade. Irrigation systems were built to control water and help crops grow in dry areas.

Mesopotamians developed a base-60 number system, which is why we use 60 minutes in an hour and 360 degrees in a circle.
